Copy Cat Chipotle Chicken

This is a recipe for a all the Chipotle lovers not wanting to spend hundreds a week on their favorite bowl/burritos. I will be showing you how to make a delicious copy-cat recipe of the famous chipotle chicken without breaking your bank. For all the meal preppers, this recipe will be the one you'll return to multiple times a month. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 1-2 lbs de-boned skinless Chicken Thighs
  • 1 can of La Costena Chipotle peppers in adobo sauce
  • 2 tbsp Extra Virgin Olive Oil
  • 1 tsp Onion powder
  • 1 tsp Dried oregano
  • 1 tsp Cumin
  • 1 tbsp Chilli Powder
  • 1-2 tsp Salt
    • optional
  • 1 tsp black pepper

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Add 1 can of chipotle peppers in adobo sauce to a blender and blend well
  2. Add 1 cup of blended sauce onto a mixing bowl
  3. Add 2 tbsp of olive oil accompanied by the onion powder,dried oregano,cumin,chilli powder,and salt
  4. Mix well
  5. Add the chicken thighs and coat well
    6. optional - allow to marinate in fridge for at least 30 min to a full 24 hours.
  6. Add 2 tbsp of olive oil to a cooking pan on medium-high heat
  7. Cook for 5-6 minutes on each side or until temperature is 170-175 degreed F.
