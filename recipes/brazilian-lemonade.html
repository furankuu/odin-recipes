BRAZILIAN LEMONADE

This is going to be a refreshing recipe. So easy to make you're fridge will be stocked throughout the week. A staple drink in the beautiful land of Brazil right at home. The sweetness and consistency of the beverage will have you wanting more. As the Brazilians say, Saude!

INGREDIENTS

  • 5 limes, cut into wedges
  • 6 cups cold water
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup sweetened condensed milk
  • ice
  • fresh limes, for serving

INSTRUCTIONS

  1. Add limes, water and sugar to a blender.
  2. Carefully, blend it together for 5-10 seconds on low. Note: Do not over mix or it will get very bitter. It will be chunky.
  3. Pour your drink mixture through a fine mesh strainer over a large bowl. Discard the lime pulp that is left over in the strainer.
  4. After rinsing the blender, add the strained lime juice back in.
  5. Now add the sweetened condensed milk to the blender. Then pulse 1-3 times until nice and frothy (do not blend too long).
  6. Pour mixture over ice in a glass.
  7. Garnish with additional fresh limes, if you are using them, serve, and enjoy.
